## 0. A single self-contained study

ORB is a single **breakout/continuation** mechanism (break of the opening range). Not a directional dichotomy (a symmetric long/short break). Method choices (window length, buffer, a volume filter) are non-owed variants (§7).

## 1. Hypothesis (falsifiable)

On the frozen large-cap **panel** at 5-min bars, trading the break of the first-N-minute opening range produces a **positive net-of-cost edge** clearing the **two-part panel verdict** (aggregate seven-point gate AND breadth), net of the full round-trip cost. **Falsified if** the panel verdict is KILL/INSUFFICIENT, or the §6 pre-check finds the median gross per-trade move below cost.

## 2. Economic rationale (mechanism, not pattern) — PURELY a-priori

The first `opening_range_minutes` of the session form the day's **initial balance** — the opening auction's price-discovery range. A decisive break of that range signals the day's **directional resolution** as the auction trends away from balance toward a new level. Trade the break. Purely a-priori.

## 3. Spec (event → entry → exit/holding → position)

In [`src/lab/research/strategies/opening_range_breakout.py`](../../src/lab/research/strategies/opening_range_breakout.py). `OpeningRangeBreakoutSpec` (`opening_range_breakout`), symmetric, one entry per day:

- **Event:** the opening range (running high/low over the first `opening_range_minutes`, then fixed) has **closed**.
- **Entry:** first bar with `close > OR_high·(1+break_buffer)` ⇒ LONG, `close < OR_low·(1−break_buffer)` ⇒ SHORT.
- **Exit/holding:** ride to the MIS square-off (sparse — adapter forward-fills, resets flat next open).

**Causal / no-lookahead (hard precondition).** `opening_range` is point-in-time (running, then fixed); entries fire only **after** the window closes (`timestamp ≥ session_open + window`), comparing to the fixed range. Signal at bar `i` uses only bars `≤ i`, fills at `i+1`'s open. **Prefix-invariance no-lookahead test** (`tests/unit/test_opening_range_breakout_spec.py::test_opening_range_breakout_prefix_invariance`) is a **hard precondition** — study does not run if it fails. Confirmed **PASSED**. **Distinct** from P3.2 (prior-N-bar intraday range) and P3.6 (global multi-session Donchian): the level is the OPENING range specifically.
