## 0. Both variants owed (directional dichotomy)

VWAP is a genuine **directional dichotomy** (`MASTER_BLUEPRINT.md:320`, "cross / reversion"). Per the dual-variant rule, **both directions are owed** and are pre-registered here as two distinct falsifiable hypotheses, each judged once by the same unchanged panel gate:

- **V1 — Reversion (fade to VWAP):** price stretched from VWAP **reverts**.
- **V2 — Cross (trend on a decisive cross):** price that **decisively crosses** VWAP **continues** in the cross direction.

**Stated ahead of the result:** V1 and V2 are near-opposite bets on the same phenomenon (behaviour around VWAP). If **both** clear the gate that is a **contradiction to investigate, not two wins** — the same phenomenon cannot robustly both revert and continue net-of-cost. The pilot's V1 (single-symbol, pre-fix) KILLed on direction (win rate 0.48; price continued from stretched levels) — **mild prior interest in V2, not licence to expect it to pass.** Params below are **blind**; the gate rules.

**V1 also doubles as the rebuild regression check:** a known-KILL under the corrected pipeline confirms the fixed machinery end-to-end. A materially different V1 verdict must be investigated before any new result is trusted.

## 1. Hypothesis (falsifiable) — per variant

On the frozen large-cap **panel** at 5-minute bars, each variant produces a **positive net-of-cost edge** clearing the **two-part panel verdict** (aggregate seven-point gate on the equal-weight portfolio AND breadth: median per-symbol CPCV path-Sharpe > 1.0 and ≥ 60% of symbols individually positive), net of the full Indian round-trip cost, realized-frequency annualized.

**Falsified if** the panel verdict is KILL/INSUFFICIENT, or the cost-viability pre-check finds the median gross per-trade move below the modeled round-trip cost.

## 2. Economic rationale (mechanism, not pattern)

VWAP is the intraday **execution benchmark** institutions size and grade fills against; VWAP-target algorithms supply mean-reverting liquidity (buy below, sell above their running benchmark).
- **V1 (reversion):** that benchmark-driven liquidity should make **extreme deviations from VWAP partially revert**. The open question is whether the reversion beats the ~0.12–0.20% round-trip cost.
- **V2 (cross):** a **decisive** cross of VWAP on real participation marks a shift in the session's fair-value consensus — resting benchmark liquidity on the far side is absorbed — so price **continues** past VWAP. The crux is distinguishing a decisive cross from whipsaw *at* VWAP (the `cross_threshold` band + holding to square-off).

## 3. Specs (event → entry → exit/holding → position)

- **V1 — `VwapMeanReversionSpec`** ([`src/lab/research/strategies/vwap.py`](../../src/lab/research/strategies/vwap.py), registered `vwap_mean_reversion`): VWAP deviation `d = close/VWAP − 1`; from flat, `d > +entry` ⇒ SHORT, `d < −entry` ⇒ LONG; hold until `|d| ≤ exit` (wide-entry / narrow-exit hysteresis), or 15:20 square-off. Point-in-time (intraday-cumulative VWAP, daily reset). **Already implemented + registered.**
- **V2 — `VwapCrossSpec`** (TO IMPLEMENT before its run — the owed cross variant): from flat, a **decisive upside cross** (`d` rises from `≤ 0` to `> +cross_threshold`) ⇒ **LONG**; a decisive downside cross (`d` falls from `≥ 0` to `< −cross_threshold`) ⇒ **SHORT**; **ride the continuation** until the opposite decisive cross flips it or 15:20 square-off. Each day opens flat; point-in-time (same VWAP; a signal at `t` fills at `t+1` open). The `cross_threshold` band suppresses whipsaw at VWAP. Register as `vwap_cross` in `lab.research.strategies.registry` with the blind params below.
