## 2. Economic rationale (mechanism, not pattern) — PURELY a-priori

A large overnight gap reflects genuine **overnight information** (earnings, global moves, news). When the gap is **confirmed by early participation** (elevated volume — real money, not a thin print) AND price **holds on the gap side of the intraday VWAP** (buyers/sellers remain in control rather than fading the open), the repricing continues intraday as latecomers chase. Trade **with** the gap. Purely a-priori: no reference to any prior result.

## 3. Spec (event → entry → exit/holding → position)

In [`src/lab/research/strategies/gap_and_go.py`](../../src/lab/research/strategies/gap_and_go.py). `GapAndGoSpec` (`gap_and_go`), symmetric, one entry per day, intraday:

- **Event:** within the first `open_window` bars, the overnight `gap` qualifies (`|gap| ≥ gap_threshold`), `relative_volume ≥ vol_mult` (participation), and price is on the gap side of the intraday `vwap`.
- **Entry:** gap up + `close > VWAP` ⇒ LONG; gap down + `close < VWAP` ⇒ SHORT (first qualifying bar).
- **Exit/holding:** ride to the MIS square-off (sparse — the adapter forward-fills the entry to the day's end, resets flat next open). Fixed unit size.

**Causal / no-lookahead (hard precondition).** `gap` is known at the open (prior-day close + today's open), `vwap` is the intraday cumulative average, `relative_volume` is trailing — all point-in-time, so the signal at bar `i` uses only bars `≤ i` and fills at `i+1`'s open. The spec has a **prefix-invariance no-lookahead test** (`tests/unit/test_gap_and_go_spec.py::test_gap_and_go_prefix_invariance`) — a **hard precondition: the study does not run if it fails.** Confirmed **PASSED**.
