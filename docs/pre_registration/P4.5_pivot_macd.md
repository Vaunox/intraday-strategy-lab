## 2. Economic rationale (mechanism, not pattern) — PURELY a-priori

A **level + momentum trigger** should reduce acting on dead levels: a bounce at support (S1) or rejection at resistance (R1) is likelier real when a **MACD crossover** confirms momentum is turning *at that moment*. Purely a-priori; honest caution — **weak priors on both legs** (pivots' weak base, MACD lag); the combination is unlikely to manufacture a real edge.
