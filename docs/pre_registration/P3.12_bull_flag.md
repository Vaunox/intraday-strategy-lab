## 0. A single self-contained study — and an honest surface flag

## 1. Hypothesis (falsifiable)

On the frozen large-cap **panel** at 5-min bars, a **completed bull/bear flag** (sharp impulse, tight low-volume consolidation, breakout) produces a **positive net-of-cost edge** clearing the **two-part panel verdict**, net of cost. **Falsified if** the panel verdict is KILL/INSUFFICIENT, or the §6 pre-check finds the median gross per-trade move below cost.

## 2. Economic rationale (mechanism, not pattern) — PURELY a-priori

A **sharp directional impulse** marks committed buyers; a **shallow, tight consolidation** afterwards is profit-taking **without real selling** (a pause, not a reversal); the move **resumes** when price breaks out of the consolidation as sidelined buyers re-enter. The tightness of the pause is the signal it is a flag, not a top. Purely a-priori.

## 3. Spec (event → entry → exit/holding → position)

In [`src/lab/research/strategies/bull_flag.py`](../../src/lab/research/strategies/bull_flag.py). `BullFlagSpec` (`bull_flag`), symmetric. At bar `i`, over three contiguous **same-day** windows:

- **Impulse:** `impulse_return = close[i-M] / close[i-M-K] - 1` (the `K`-bar move ending `M` bars ago) with `|impulse_return| ≥ impulse_threshold`.
- **Consolidation:** the prior `M` bars `[i-M .. i-1]` (`prior_donchian`, excludes the current bar) are **tight**: `consol_range ≤ tight_frac · |impulse price move|`.
- **Breakout:** `close[i]` beyond the consolidation extreme (`> consol_high` bull / `< consol_low` bear) ⇒ entry; ride to the MIS square-off (sparse — adapter forward-fills, resets flat next open).

**Same-day guard:** all three windows must lie within one IST day, so an **overnight gap can never masquerade as the impulse** (that would make this a gap-and-go, P3.10).

**Causal / no-lookahead (hard precondition).** `prior_donchian` and indexed closes are point-in-time; signal at bar `i` uses only bars `≤ i`, fills at `i+1`'s open. **Prefix-invariance no-lookahead test** (`tests/unit/test_bull_flag_spec.py::test_bull_flag_prefix_invariance`) is a **hard precondition** — study does not run if it fails. Confirmed **PASSED**.
