Gap direction is a **both-owed dichotomy** (operator ruling 2026-07-10; the settled list gains gap-and-go/gap-fade, joining P3.1/P3.7/P3.13). ## 1. Hypothesis (falsifiable)

On the frozen large-cap **panel** at 5-minute bars, fading an opening gap that **rejects back through VWAP** (fails to hold the gap side) toward the gap fill produces a **positive net-of-cost edge** clearing the **two-part panel verdict** (aggregate seven-point gate AND breadth: median per-symbol CPCV path-Sharpe > 1.0 and ≥ 60% positive), net of the full Indian round-trip cost, realized-frequency annualized. **Falsified if** the panel verdict is KILL/INSUFFICIENT, or the §6 pre-check finds the median gross per-trade move below cost.

## 2. Economic rationale (mechanism, not pattern) — PURELY a-priori

A large gap that **cannot hold** — price rejecting back through the intraday VWAP despite the gap and participation — signals the gap was an **overreaction / liquidity event being faded**: the marginal buyer (of a gap up) is exhausted, sellers reclaim VWAP, and price **mean-reverts toward the prior close** (the "gap fill"). Fade **against** the gap once rejection confirms. This is the *opposite* mechanism to gap-and-go (which needs the gap to *hold*), and it matters **most exactly when continuation fails** — which is why it is owed, not gated on the continuation showing life. Purely a-priori.

## 3. Spec (event → entry → exit/holding → position)

In [`src/lab/research/strategies/gap_fade.py`](../../src/lab/research/strategies/gap_fade.py). `GapFadeSpec` (`gap_fade`), symmetric, one entry per day, intraday — **mirrors `GapAndGoSpec`, with the VWAP-side condition flipped**:

- **Event:** within the first `open_window` bars, the overnight `gap` qualifies (`|gap| ≥ gap_threshold`), `relative_volume ≥ vol_mult` (participation), and price has **rejected to the opposite side** of the intraday `vwap`.
- **Entry:** gap up + `close < VWAP` ⇒ **SHORT** (fade down toward the fill); gap down + `close > VWAP` ⇒ **LONG** (fade up toward the fill) — first qualifying bar.
- **Exit/holding:** ride to the MIS square-off (sparse — the adapter forward-fills, resets flat next open). Fixed unit size.

**Causal / no-lookahead (hard precondition).** `gap` (known at the open), `vwap` (intraday cumulative), `relative_volume` (trailing) are all point-in-time; the signal at bar `i` uses only bars `≤ i`, fills at `i+1`'s open. **Prefix-invariance no-lookahead test** (`tests/unit/test_gap_fade_spec.py::test_gap_fade_prefix_invariance`) — a **hard precondition** — Confirmed **PASSED**.

**Divergence from the twin — PROVEN (both-owed precondition).** On real RELIANCE data (2,816 sessions), `gap_fade` fires **226 entries** and `gap_and_go` **189**, with **0 shared entry bars (0.0% overlap)** — the twins fire on perfectly disjoint bars (reject vs hold). This is a **genuine** dichotomy, not the degenerate P3.7 KAMA case; the divergence test (`test_gap_fade_diverges_from_gap_and_go`) also asserts it on a fixture.
