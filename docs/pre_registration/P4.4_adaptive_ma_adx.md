## 0. Blueprint combination

Blueprint P4.4 (RESEARCH_FINDINGS §4.2): *take the KAMA/AMA trend signal only when ADX confirms sufficient trend strength (gate out chop).* A minimal **AND** of the KAMA trend (P3.7) and an ADX strength gate, **fewest knobs**. ADX is direction-agnostic (strength only) → a reinforcing filter, not mutually-exclusive.

## 1. Hypothesis (falsifiable)

On the frozen large-cap **panel** at 5-min bars, holding the KAMA-slope trend **only when ADX confirms a trend** produces a **positive net-of-cost edge** clearing the **two-part panel verdict**. **Falsified if** KILL/INSUFFICIENT.

## 2. Economic rationale (mechanism, not pattern) — PURELY a-priori

Trade the trend rule **only when the trend is real**: gating the KAMA direction by ADX > threshold should cut the whipsaw that killed the naked adaptive-trend signal in chop. Trend-strength gating is one of the more defensible filters. Purely a-priori; honest caution — ADX **lags**, gating **shrinks the sample**, and it is two tools (more params); the net intraday edge is unproven.

## 3. Spec (event → entry → exit/holding → position)

In [`src/lab/research/strategies/adaptive_ma_adx.py`](../../src/lab/research/strategies/adaptive_ma_adx.py). `AdaptiveMaAdxSpec`, symmetric, per-bar: hold the KAMA **slope** side (LONG rising / SHORT falling) **only when `ADX > adx_threshold`**; flat when ADX is weak. Square-off each day.
